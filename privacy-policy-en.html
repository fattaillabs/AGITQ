Privacy Policy

AGITQ Playbook

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Summary: AGITQ Playbook does not directly collect any personal information. However, third-party advertising providers may collect certain information for ad services.

1. Introduction

AGITQ Playbook (the "App") takes the protection of your personal information very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Directly by the App

AGITQ Playbook does not directly collect any personal information.

All data you enter in the App (portfolio information, settings, etc.) is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

2.2 Information Collected by Third Parties (Advertising)

The App includes third-party advertising services to provide a free service. Advertising providers may collect the following information for ad delivery and analytics:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions, etc.)

3. Purpose of Information Use

Information collected by third parties (Google AdMob) is used for the following purposes:

  • Delivering personalized advertisements
  • Measuring and analyzing ad performance
  • Fraud prevention and security

4. Ad Tracking Settings

You can limit ad tracking in your iOS settings:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking to manage app tracking requests.
  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising to turn off personalized ads.

5. Data Storage

All data used in the App (portfolio, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the App will also delete all associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notices or update notes.
